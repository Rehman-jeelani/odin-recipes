Mint Margarita

Description

Mint Margarita is a refreshing, zesty drink made from fresh mint leaves, lemon or lime juice, sugar, and ice, blended to create a cool, tangy beverage. Often served chilled with a salted rim, it strikes the perfect balance between sweet, sour, and herbal flavors. Popular in South Asia, especially Pakistan, it’s a non-alcoholic twist on the classic margarita—ideal for summer afternoons, iftar, or as a welcome drink at gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (or lime juice)
  • 2–3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1½ cups cold water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • A pinch of black salt (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon roasted cumin powder (optional)
  • Salt for rimming the glass (optional)
  • Lemon slices or mint sprigs for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the mint leaves thoroughly and set aside.
  2. In a blender, combine mint leaves, lemon juice, sugar, and cold water.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Taste and adjust sugar or lemon juice if needed.
  5. Optional: Strain the mixture for a smoother texture.
  6. Optional: Rim serving glasses with lemon juice and dip in salt for a classic look.
  7. Pour the blended drink into glasses.
  8. Garnish with a slice of lemon or a mint sprig.
  9. Serve immediately while chilled and refreshing.
